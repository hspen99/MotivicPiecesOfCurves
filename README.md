# MotivicPiecesOfCurves
Code associated to the pre-print `Motivic pieces of curves: L-functions and periods' (https://hspen99.github.io/MotivicPieces.pdf)

Dihedral.m: comparison of L-function of motivic pieces and built-in L-series of classical newform (Example 4.9)

NOTE: Superelliptic computations below require the package of Molin and Neurohr found at https://github.com/pascalmolin/hcperiods.

Superelliptic_Exp3.m: computation of L-value and periods of motivic pieces of Picard curves (Example 6.3)

Superelliptic_Exp4.m: computation of L-value and periods of motivic pieces of genus 3, exponent 4 superelliptic curves (Table 1)
